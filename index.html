Projects

Indoor Motion Capture System for Aerial Drones

Trajectory tracking and payload carrying drone has been demonstrated under Optitrack motion capture system.

Fast Planner with Octomapping Obstacle Avoidance for Drones

Fast-Planner (HKUST) is developed aiming to enable quadrotor fast flight in complex unknown environments. Intel NUC, PX4 and Realsense RGBD Camera has been used for outdoor navigation.

Ewok: Real-Time Trajectory Replanning for MAVs using Uniform B-splines and 3D Circular Buffer

Ewok is for autonmous navigation of drones while avoiding the obstacles. It uses local mapping technique and targeted waypoints are replanned at every timestamp.

Autonomous Safe Landing of Quadcopters

A quadcopter having a down facing camera will detect obstacle-free surface and land there safely. PX4 Autopilot - SITL package has been used for Gazebo and Rviz simulation .

Coordinated motion of UGV and a UAV while carrying a payload

Coordinated motion of heterogeneous robot groups are more appealing due to the fact that unique advantages of different robots might be combined to increase the overall efficiency of the system. A Unmanned Ground Vehicles (UGVs) is tracking a predefined path and an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is following the UGV. A delivery exchange has been demonstrated to compare the performance with and without payload.

Model Predictive Control for Multi-Robot Trajectory Tracking

A ROS and gazebo simulation video in which autonomous platoon formation control is developed using Model Preidctive Controller for an fixed trajectory tracking.

Co-operative multi-robot payload carrying system on uneven surface

a multi robot payload transport system to carry payloads through an environment of unknown and uneven inclinations while maintaining the desired orientation of the payload. For this task, we used custom built robots with a linear actuator (pistons mounted on top of each robot. The system continuously monitors the payload’s orientation and computes the required piston height of each robot to maintain the desired orientation of the payload.

Co-operative multi-robot dynamic formation control

Leader-follower formation control algorithm has been used for the coordination of multiple agents for maintaining a formation. Robots' formation can shrink and expand according to it's environment.

Arduino based waypoint tracking using GPS and Magnetometer.

Pre-defined Latitude and Longitude is tracked using GPS and Magnetometer, while avoiding the obstacles between the waypoints. GPS Data is used to find "Target Heading" and Magnetometer Placed on robot give us "Current Heading" of robot. And difference beteern "Target and Current Heading" will decide angular velocity of robot.
